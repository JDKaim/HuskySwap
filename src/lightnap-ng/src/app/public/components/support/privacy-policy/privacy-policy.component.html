Privacy Policy

Last updated December 2, 2024.

HuskySwap ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our platform, which facilitates class swaps for students at the University of Washington ("UW").

By accessing or using HuskySwap, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide Directly

- Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and other information you choose to provide.

- Class Listings: Information about the classes you list for swapping, including course details and preferences.

- Messages: Any communications you send through our platform.

b. Information We Collect Automatically

- Device and Usage Data: We collect information about your device, browser type, IP address, and interactions with our platform.

- Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and collect data about your usage of our platform.

c. Information from Third Parties

- We may receive information from UW systems or other authorized institutions to verify your student status.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide and improve our Services.

- Facilitate class swaps by displaying relevant information to other users.

- Communicate with you, including sending notifications about potential swaps or platform updates.

- Ensure compliance with UW policies and applicable laws.

- Analyze platform usage to enhance functionality and user experience.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

- With Other Users: Limited information, such as your first name and class listings, is visible to other users for the purpose of facilitating class swaps.

- With Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as hosting or analytics.

- For Legal Reasons: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or if we believe such action is necessary to:

--- Comply with legal obligations.

--- Protect and defend our rights or property.

--- Prevent fraud or other illegal activity.

- With Your Consent: We may share your information for other purposes with your explicit consent.

4. Your Privacy Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

- Access and Update: You can access and update your account information through your profile settings.

- Delete Your Account: You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at jdkaim@uw.edu

- Opt-Out of Communications: You can opt out of non-essential communications by updating your notification preferences.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as your account is active or as necessary to provide our Services. If you request account deletion, we will delete your information, except where retention is required for legal or regulatory purposes.

6. Cookies and Tracking

HuskySwap uses cookies to:

- Authenticate users and maintain session data.

- Understand how users interact with our platform.

- Provide personalized experiences.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect your experience on our platform.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login credentials.

8. Children's Privacy

HuskySwap is intended for use by UW students. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be effective upon posting to the platform, and your continued use of the Services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

- Email: jdkaim@uw.edu

- Address: HuskySwap Team, University of Washington, Seattle, WA

11. Conclusion

By using HuskySwap, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
